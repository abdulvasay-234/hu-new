What is OpenBuild Week?

HackUnion's premium builder week for practical collaboration.

OpenBuild Week is where ambitious people build together in public, exchange high-signal knowledge, and convert ideas into shipped outcomes through guided sessions and community collaboration.

Mission

Enable builders to create meaningful, real-world technology projects through structured collaboration, mentorship, and open participation.

Vision

Become the most trusted, community-first build week model that empowers emerging and experienced creators to learn, ship, and lead together.

Who Should Attend

  • Developers
  • AI Engineers
  • Designers
  • Founders
  • Students
  • Open Source Contributors
  • Technology Communities

Why It Matters

It turns isolated effort into shared momentum, creating stronger portfolios, better products, deeper community trust, and faster growth for everyone involved.
