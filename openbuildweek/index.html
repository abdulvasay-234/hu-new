What is OpenBuild Week?

HackUnion's flagship builder experience for students.

Open Build Week is HackUnion's flagship builder experience where students learn, build, collaborate, and showcase real-world projects while connecting with mentors, industry professionals, and fellow builders.

🚀 Build Real Projects

Turn ideas into real-world projects, strengthen your portfolio, and gain practical experience by building with other passionate creators.

🏆 Win & Get Recognized

Compete for a ₹30,000 Prize Pool, earn certificates, exclusive goodies, and showcase your work to the community.

💼 Internship & Placement Opportunities

Top-performing builders get an opportunity to connect with our hiring partners for internships and career opportunities.

🤝 Build Together

Collaborate with developers, designers, AI enthusiasts, founders, and builders who love creating and solving real problems.

🎓 Learn from Experts

Gain insights through expert talks, mentorship sessions, hands-on workshops, and live demonstrations from industry professionals.

❤️ Builder Spirit

Build in public.
Learn by doing.
Share your knowledge.
Support fellow builders.
Grow together.
Because the best products are built by communities, not individuals.
