Community Guidelines

Code of Conduct

A welcoming community is built by everyone.

HackUnion is committed to creating a safe, inclusive, respectful, and collaborative environment where everyone can learn, build, and grow together.

Report an Issue Contact Organizers

Why this matters

Great communities are built on trust, respect, and accountability.

This page explains the standards we follow and how we respond when concerns are raised.

TL;DR

  • 🤝 Respect everyone
  • 🌍 Welcome everyone
  • 💡 Learn and share
  • 🚀 Build together
  • ❤️ Support one another
  • 🚫 No harassment, bullying, discrimination, or hate speech
  • 🎯 Keep discussions constructive
  • 📢 Report issues to organizers

In short: Be kind, be respectful, and help us build an amazing community together.

Our Pledge

Our Pledge

HackUnion is committed to making participation in our community a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, ethnicity, disability, gender identity, background, education, nationality, religion, experience level, or any other personal characteristic.

We are building a community where every builder can learn openly, collaborate safely, and contribute with confidence.

Our Standards

Our Standards

Positive Behaviour

  • Respect everyone
  • Show empathy
  • Welcome newcomers
  • Share knowledge
  • Give constructive feedback
  • Accept feedback gracefully
  • Encourage learning
  • Build together
  • Support fellow builders
  • Celebrate others' success

Unacceptable Behaviour

  • Harassment
  • Hate speech
  • Bullying
  • Discrimination
  • Sexual harassment
  • Personal attacks
  • Trolling
  • Publishing private information
  • Threats
  • Violence
  • Offensive content
  • Spam
  • Doxxing
  • Deliberately disrupting events

This list is not exhaustive. Any behavior that harms safety, trust, or participation may lead to action.

Responsibilities

Responsibilities

Organizers, moderators, volunteers, and community leaders are responsible for maintaining a welcoming, respectful, and inclusive environment across all HackUnion spaces.

Reporting

Reporting

If you experience or witness behavior that violates this Code of Conduct, please report it to the HackUnion organizing team as soon as possible.

Reports are handled respectfully and confidentially whenever possible. We do not tolerate retaliation against anyone who raises a concern in good faith.

Enforcement

Enforcement

Step 1

Correction

We start with a friendly reminder and practical guidance so members can correct behavior quickly.

Community Impact: Helps restore respectful interaction early.

Possible Consequence: Verbal or written correction with clear expectations.

Step 2

Warning

If behavior continues or is more serious, a formal warning is issued and documented.

Community Impact: Protects trust and signals accountability.

Possible Consequence: Official warning and close monitoring.

Step 3

Temporary Suspension

Access may be paused for a defined period from events or community spaces to prevent further harm.

Community Impact: Creates breathing room and improves safety.

Possible Consequence: Temporary removal from sessions, channels, or activities.

Step 4

Permanent Removal

Severe or repeated violations may result in a permanent ban from HackUnion activities.

Community Impact: Preserves long-term safety and community standards.

Possible Consequence: Permanent removal from all official HackUnion spaces.

Every incident is reviewed individually, based on context, severity, and community safety.
