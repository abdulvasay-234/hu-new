Logo Usage

Do's and don'ts for using HackUnion marks.

Follow these simple rules to keep all HackUnion and Open Build Week communication consistent and recognizable.

Correct usage

  • Maintain clear space around the logo.
  • Use only official brand colors.
  • Preserve original proportions.

Incorrect usage

  • Do not stretch or squash the logo.
  • Do not rotate the logo.
  • Do not recolor the logo.
  • Do not add shadows, glows, or effects.
  • Do not crop or partially hide the logo.
