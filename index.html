Builder manifesto

Built for people who ship together.

HackUnion is where developers, designers, founders, AI engineers, and creators build in public through real experiences, real projects, and open collaboration.

Members join to work alongside people who care about craft, share progress early, and keep shipping long after the event ends.

  3. 03 Share the work early.
